PRODUCT HIGHLIGHT: PLASTICS INDUSTRY

EXDM Extruder Degassing Module for the Plastics Industry

Vacuum pumps play an important role in degassing plastics to remove water vapour, toxic & corrosive gases and impurities that affect the quality of the product. Polymer melts are degassed at low pressures using vacuum pumps on extruders, when being compounded into different grades with additives and colourants and while being formed. Depending on your vacuum needs, our experts will select from our comprehensive range of pumps to deliver the right vacuum system for your process.
