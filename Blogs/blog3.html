Edwards voted as one of the world’s best suppliers in the TechInsights 2024 survey

We are delighted to announce that we were again voted number one in the ‘Best Suppliers of Wafer Fabrication Equipment’ category and number three in ’10 Best Large Suppliers of Chip Making Equipment’ in 2024’s major semiconductor industry customer satisfaction survey, TechInsights.
The 2024 TechInsights Customer Satisfaction Survey is a large industry-wide survey that allows us to get a real insight into how our customers in the semiconductor industry perceive Edwards. This year over 18,000 responses were received from participants representing 53% of the chip market, and 96% of subsystem customers worldwide.
